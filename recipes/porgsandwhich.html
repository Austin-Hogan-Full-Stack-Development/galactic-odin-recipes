Pulled Porg Sandwhiches

Perfect Pulled Porg Sanwiches to eat before the Pod Races

Ingridients

  • 1 Fresh Porg
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Your choice of seasoning
  • Buns

Steps

  1. Season the Porg to your liking
  2. Simmer & Shred the Porg
  3. Cover with your favorite BBQ Sauce
  4. Place Porg on a Bun & Enjoy!
