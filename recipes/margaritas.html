Beach Day Margaritas

3 Ingridient Beach Day Margarita

Ingridients

  • Tequila
  • Fresh Lime Juice
  • Orange Liqueur - Cointreau

Steps

  1. Prep Glass. Run a lime around the rim & dip in salt.
  2. Combine Ingridients into glass with some ice cubes.
  3. Stir well!
  4. Taste & adjust to your liking.
