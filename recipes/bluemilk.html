Meiloorun Fruit & Blue Milk

Fruit & Milk, a healthy desert before bed.

Ingridients

  • 1 Fresh Meiloorun Fruit
  • Your choice of Green or Blue Thala-Sren Milk

Steps

  1. Cut the Meiloorun Fruit into wedges
  2. Pour your desired amount of milk
  3. Hop in a bed cozy enough to keep you warm on Hoth!
